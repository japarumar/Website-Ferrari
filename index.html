THE FOUNDATION OF THE FERRARI BRAND

Our story officially begins in 1947 when the first car built under the Ferrari marque, the 125 S, drove through the historic factory gates on Via Abetone Inferiore in Maranello. This marked the beginning of a legacy that would redefine automotive excellence for generations to come.
